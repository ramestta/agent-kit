What is Ramestta AI Agent OS?

In one line: it lets an AI assistant safely handle money and online tasks for you — with your rules, your limits, and an off-switch you control. Here's the whole idea, in plain language.

Show me the idea ↓ See real examples

The problem

AI can think and talk. It can't safely act.

Today's AI can write your emails and plan your day. But the moment you want it to actually do something — pay a bill, move savings, run a task every morning — you hit a wall.

🔑

You'd have to hand over your keys

To let an AI spend money, you'd give it your card number or wallet password. Now it can spend anything — that's terrifying.

🛑

No real off-switch

If the AI makes a mistake or gets hacked, there's no clean way to instantly stop it or take back control.

🕵️

You can't see what it did

Actions happen on some company's private server. You just have to trust it. No clear, tamper-proof record.

The solution

Give the AI its own safe wallet, your rulebook, and a pause button.

Ramestta AI Agent OS is a system on the blockchain that hands every AI agent a dedicated account — locked inside your rules. You decide how much it can spend, who it can pay, and what it may do. You keep the master key. It can never go beyond your limits, and you can freeze it in one click.

💳

Its own limited wallet

The agent gets a fresh wallet with a spending limit you set — like a prepaid card. It never touches your main money.

📜

Your rules are built-in

"Max ₹500/day, only pay these people, never do that." The rules live on the blockchain — the AI physically cannot break them.

⏸️

You're always in control

Watch every action on a public ledger. Pause, change limits, or shut it down instantly — anytime, from anywhere.

The simplest way to picture it

It's a debit card for your AI — with your rules and a supervisor.

You wouldn't give a new assistant your entire bank account. You'd give them a card with a limit, a list of what's allowed, and the power to freeze it. That's exactly what this does — for AI.

Everything an AI agent needs to act — safely

Booting an agent gives it six things at once. Here's what each one means, in plain words.

🏷️
A name

A readable identity like mybot.rama instead of a long random address — easy to recognise and trust.

👛
A wallet

Its own on-chain account to hold and move a limited amount of money — separate from yours.

⛽
Free-to-run gas

Blockchain actions normally cost a tiny fee; the system can sponsor these so the agent just works.

🛡️
Permissions

The rulebook: spend caps, allowed contacts, and actions that need your approval first.

⏰
A scheduler

Run tasks on time — every day, every hour — with no server and no cron job to babysit.

🔐
Private messaging

Agents can talk to each other (and to you) with end-to-end encryption.

Why it's safe

You never lose control

  • ✓
    The master key stays with youThe agent only ever uses a temporary, limited session key — never the key that owns everything.
  • ✓
    Hard spending limitsPer-transaction, per-day and per-month caps are enforced by code. The agent literally cannot exceed them.
  • ✓
    Big actions need your approvalAnything above a limit you set waits for a human "yes" before it can go through.
  • ✓
    Instant pause & revokeFreeze the agent or cancel its access in one click, from anywhere — no waiting.
  • ✓
    Everything is on the recordEvery action is written to a public blockchain you can inspect. No hidden moves.
  • ✓
    Upgrades have a safety delayThe system's own code can only change after a public 24-hour timelock — no surprise changes.
