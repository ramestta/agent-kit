● Real examples

What can you actually build with it?

Think of an agent as a tireless helper with a debit card and a rulebook. Here are everyday jobs it can do — reliably, on time, and always inside the limits you set.

For everyone ↓For businesses & builders
👤 For everyone

Money chores that just… take care of themselves

🐷

Auto-savings & steady investing

"Put ₹500 into my savings every Friday, and buy a little crypto each month." The agent does it on schedule — no reminders, no missed weeks. Great for building a habit without thinking about it.

🛡️Capped per week/month. It can never touch more than you allow.
🧾

Bill & subscription payer

"Pay my rent on the 1st and my subscriptions when they're due." On-time, every time — even if you're travelling or asleep. No late fees, no forgetting.

🛡️Only the exact recipients you approved. Unknown payees are blocked.
👨‍👩‍👧

Allowance & family transfers

"Send my kid their weekly allowance, and my parents a fixed amount monthly." Set it once; it runs itself. Everyone gets paid on time without you lifting a finger.

🛡️Fixed amounts, fixed schedule. Change or stop it anytime in one click.
🔔

Watch & react

"If this price drops below X, buy a little — and always tell me what happened." The agent watches 24/7 and acts within your rules, then reports back.

🛡️Small pre-approved actions run automatically; anything bigger waits for your "yes".
🏢 For businesses & builders

Automation that doesn't depend on a server you babysit

💼

Payroll & recurring payouts

"Pay the team every 1st and 15th." The schedule lives on-chain, so it runs even if your laptop is off — no cron job, no missed cycles, a clear public record for accounting.

🛡️Bounded budget + approvals for anything out of the ordinary.
📈

Treasury rebalancing / yield

"Keep our funds earning the best safe rate, and rebalance weekly." The agent decides; the on-chain scheduler guarantees it actually runs — inside strict, code-enforced limits.

🛡️Allowed contracts + spend caps mean it can only do the job you scoped.
🤝

A team of agents

"One agent finds the work, another executes, a third checks it." Agents coordinate over end-to-end encrypted messaging and shared on-chain memory — a small workforce, each with its own name and wallet.

🛡️Every agent is separately bounded and separately revocable.
🏛️

DAO / community operations

"Run our recurring grants and rewards without a human clicking each time." Transparent, on-time, and verifiable — the community can audit every action on-chain.

🛡️Rules + a 24-hour timelock on changes mean no surprise moves.
